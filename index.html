💡 Tips for better results:
— Try searching "Athena" and "Minerva" separately, as European museums often use Latin vs Greek names inconsistently.
— Europeana includes contributions from the Louvre, national museums of Greece, Italy, Germany, the Netherlands, and many others — but coverage varies by institution.
— For museums not covered here (British Museum, Uffizi, Naples), a separate adapter can be added to this tool.
— Always verify image rights before publishing — Europeana shows rights status per object.
